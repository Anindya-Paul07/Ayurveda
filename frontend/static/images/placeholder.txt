Add featured article images (e.g., dosha-types.jpg, seasonal-practices.jpg, etc.) to this directory.

This directory is intended for storing images used in featured articles throughout the Ayurveda application.

Recommended image formats:
- JPG/JPEG for photographs
- PNG for graphics with transparency
- SVG for vector graphics and icons

Suggested naming convention:
- Use lowercase letters
- Separate words with hyphens
- Include descriptive names related to the article content

Examples of expected images:
- dosha-types.jpg
- seasonal-practices.jpg
- ayurvedic-herbs.jpg
- meditation-techniques.png
- yoga-poses.jpg
